    About Us


    The Computer and Communication Centre is responsible for keeping the Electronics and Communication as well as Computation related facilities available to each and every member of IIIT Bhagalpur.

    The services provided by the Computer and Communication Centre includes:

    Installation and Maintenance of Servers for:

  • Internet Access
  • E-mailing Facilities
  • Computation Facilities.
  • Management and upkeep of the Official IIIT Bhagalpur and Intranet Web Page.

    • The Centre is having DELL PowerEdge R730 Servers of Max Turbo Speed @2.20 GHz with 12 cores and 64 GB RAM running CentOS Operating System.

    The campus LAN connection of Academic and Administrative building has been extended to both boy's and girl's hostel. On campus, students can access high-speed LAN and Wi-fi. IIIT Bhagalpur is part of National Knowledge Network (NKN), which ensures 100 Mbps connection which will be upgarded to 1 Gbps very soon.
